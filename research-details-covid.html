UNRAVELING IMPLICIT HUMAN BEHAVIORAL EFFECTS ON DYNAMIC CHARACTERISTICS OF COVID-19 DAILY INFECTION RATES IN TAIWAN

Research information

  • Author: Ting-Li Chen, Elizabeth Chou, Min-Yi Chen, and Fushing Hsieh
  • Journal: PLoS One (under review)
  • Project date: Jun, 2022 ~ Nov, 2022
  • Project URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.10926
  • Shiny URL: https://ccminyi.shinyapps.io/Covid_daily_infection_rate_in_TW/

This is a research project in cooperation with Dr. Ting-Li Chen, Dr. Elizabeth Chou, and Dr. Fushing Hsieh. We are studying the dynamic curves of the spread of COVID-19 in Taiwan using computational developments based on theoretical information measurements of conditional entropy and mutual information.
