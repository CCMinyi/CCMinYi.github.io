Project information

  • Group Members: Min-Yi Chen, Tsai-Chen Hsieh, Tsun-Chun Yang, Chun-Hao Wang
  • Class: Data Science @ NCCU
  • Advisor: Jia-Ming Chang
  • Project date: Oct, 2020 ~ Jan, 2021
  • Project URL: RShiny

In the Data Science class at NCCU, under Professor Jia-Ming Chang's guidance, we conducted a group project analyzing winning factors and predicting match outcomes using Decision Trees, Naive Bayes, Random Forest, and SVM algorithms. Our performance was evaluated through cross-validation, accuracy, AUC, and other metrics. We showcased our results using interactive visualizations in R and Shiny. Our project was voted the best among 12 groups in the class.
